Key Features

User Registration & Profile

Create an account and manage your profile to keep track of your lost and found items.

Item Post

Easily post details about lost or found items to help others reconnect with their belongings.

Search & Filter

Efficiently search and filter through lost and found items to locate what you're looking for.

About Us

Lost & Found was inspired by our own experiences of losing valuable items and struggling to find them. We wanted to create a platform that makes it easier for people to reunite with their belongings. This project is part of our Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
